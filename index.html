Dinosaur Encyclopedia

All known dinosaurs come from the super order Dinosauria, and are divided into two major orders: Saurischia, and Ornithischia. Therapods and Sauropods derive from the same order - Saurischia - though they quickly diverged into distinct evolutionary paths; becoming the clades we see them as: Sauropods and Therapods. Ornithischia is the other order under Dinosauria and makes up a lot of dinosaur diversity.
